Our Roots

Cutters Choice Radio is the wayward, rebellious offspring of station boss Benny Fonzarelli. Back in the summer of 2011, Benny—a young punk from Wycombe—teamed up with Xavi from Barcelona to scratch that creative itch. That band was called Cutters Choice, and it was only the beginning.

The Mad Women’s Shit Sessions

What started as a once-a-week punk-fuelled experiment grew into a full-blown radio revolution—and “Traffic Island Dicks” became legend. DJs like Chester Bothworlds, West Ham Dave, Rolex Tharsus, and Burns Van De Graaf joined the party, and every Wednesday has been CCR night ever since.

The Unofficial Isolation Station

March 2020: Covid-19 gate-crashed the party, but CCR rose like a beer-sodden phoenix. Country Cockney took the mic, broadcasting marathon lockdown sets that hooked listeners worldwide—and earned himself a permanent seat at the table.

Take It to the Next Level

Country Cockney’s recruitment push brought fresh jocks into the fold, and CCR’s roster—and listener base—exploded. Soon he was not just spinning tunes, but steering the ship as a full partner in Cutters Choice Radio.

International DJs

Now you’ll hear DJs from New Zealand, Australia, Norway, USA, Canada, Spain—and beyond. A truly global collective, united by one rule: anything goes.

Radio Cult

In 2024 we made the leap to Radio Cult’s 320 kbps stream. There were tech hiccups, sure—but for “significant bangers,” nothing but the best will do.

Relentless Flavas, Significant Bangers

Today CCR is 24/7 quality mayhem, with over 40 DJs covering genres from jungle to footwork to UK hardcore—all on one fearless station.
