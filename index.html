Make yourself at home…

We are an open-minded and musically diverse radio station, fast approaching our 10th year of broadcasting. With over 40 experienced DJs bringing you a wide range of quality shows covering every genre thinkable.

Our Roots

Cutters Choice Radio is the rebellious offspring of station boss Benny Fonzarelli. In summer 2011, Benny—a punk from Wycombe—teamed up with Xavi from Barcelona to scratch that creative itch. That band was Cutters Choice, and the journey began.

The Mad Women’s Shit Sessions

What started as a once-a-week punk experiment grew into a radio revolution—“Traffic Island Dicks” became legend as DJs Chester Bothworlds, West Ham Dave, Rolex Tharsus, and Burns Van De Graaf joined the party.

The Unofficial Isolation Station

March 2020: Covid-19 gate-crashed the party, but CCR rose like a beer-sodden phoenix. Country Cockney’s marathon lockdown sets hooked listeners worldwide—and earned him a permanent seat at the table.

Take It to the Next Level

Country Cockney’s recruitment drive brought fresh jocks into the fold. CCR’s roster—and listener base—exploded, and he became a full partner in Cutters Choice Radio.

International DJs

Hear DJs from New Zealand, Australia, Norway, the USA, Canada, Spain—and beyond. A global collective where anything goes.

Radio Cult

In 2024 we switched to Radio Cult’s 320 kbps stream. There were tech hiccups, but for “significant bangers,” nothing less than the best will do.

Relentless Flavas, Significant Bangers

Today CCR is 24/7 quality mayhem, with 40+ DJs covering everything from jungle to footwork to UK hardcore—all under one fearless station.
